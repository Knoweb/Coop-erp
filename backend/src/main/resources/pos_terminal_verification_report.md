# Final Verification Report

## Objective
Finalize and verify terminal-wise sales history and cash closing in Coop Grocery Management System, ensuring isolation by tenant, shop, terminal, and session.

## Issues Addressed
1. **Hidden Sales Bug**: Sales generated by `ShopSaleController` were successfully persisted (triggering stock updates and session updates) but were invisible to sales history queries. This occurred because `tenant_id` was not being set on the `Sale` entity, causing Hibernate's multi-tenancy `@Filter` to silently exclude them from results.
2. **Admin Transfer Bug**: Admin-to-shop stock transfers (via `AdminSale` with `SaleType.SHOP`) were missing `tenant_id` as well.
3. **Session vs History Mismatch**: Because sales were saved but filtered out, `CashSession` totals were correctly inflating, but `getShopSalesHistoryFiltered` could not read the associated sales.
4. **Test Adjustments**: Updated the POS testing script (`test-terminal-cash-closing.ps1`) to properly use `ShopAdmin` for stock initialization and `Admin` for stock transfers, validating the complete POS pipeline.

## Fixes Implemented
- In `SaleService.java`, injected `TenantRepository`.
- For `createShopSale`, correctly assigned the tenant from the source shop: `sale.setTenant(sourceShop.getTenant());`.
- For `createAdminSale`, correctly assigned the tenant via `TenantContext` and `TenantRepository`.
- Successfully re-ran `test-terminal-cash-closing.ps1` end-to-end to confirm both cash closing and sales history logic.
